Psychic Spy

Code Name: Razum
Filename: Katia Federov
Specialty: Remote viewing, espionage
Birthplace: Vanavara, Russia

Razum is a product of the Babochka remote viewing program, a black ops project left over from Soviet Russia. The program was recently discovered and terminated, but not before the agents were sold off to the merchants of war.

Razum has the bad habit of drowning out her thoughts with alcohol. When she gets inhibriated she has a cruel tendency to read people's minds and betray their secrets to their entourage.
