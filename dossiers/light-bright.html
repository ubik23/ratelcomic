Influencer

Code Name: Light Bright
Filename: Debbie-Ann Doherty
Specialty: Being at the Wrong Place at the Right Time
Birthplace: Plateau Mont-Royal

A young aspiring influencer and all around nuissance, Debbie-Ann chases clicks and clout, hoping to game the algorythm in her favor to helps her realize her nebulous ambitions of going viral and becoming famous.

She has more enthousiasm than ability in this world, luckily she is surrounded by highly skilled friends who take a liking to her naïvité and natural exuberance.

Her youthful naïvité and love for life is often contagious to those around her that she has no difficulty in making friends in strange places. A lot of them follow her on social media and watch her struggle to find the key to her success.

In Ratel she finds a potential source of fame and recognition if she can track him down and reveal his identity to the world. She has little regard to the consequences for others and this could blowback in her face and crush her dreams of Internet fame.

"She's cute, sure, but when she gets going, man is she annoying" an old school friend
