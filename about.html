Ratel (french for "honey badger") is an independant Superhero comic book project created by Frederic Guimont in december 2022 and the first issue of the comic series will be coming out in 2024 (The launch date will be announced when the artwork nears the end of completion). Stay updated on the development with our up to date progress page. I will be updating the numbers on that page as the work is completed.

The comic will be available for preorder shortly through crowdfunding on FMC as well as on Geyser. The marketing campaign is being worked on at the moment and will be announced once everything is ready to go.

Updates will be announced on this site as well as on our mailing list. Join today to be the first in line for certain perks.

About the author

Frédéric Guimont is a French Canadian born in Germany, grew up an army brat and learned to draw from books. One day, while dragging a pencil against the wall by the eraser, the lead pierced into his hand, breakingi off a piece of graphite into his palm. Nurses at the school tried to remove to no avail. The graphite seeped into his blood stream, giving him super-penciler skills.

He worked on a little known comic titled Candy Apple Black in the early two thousands, adapted a few chapters of Orwell's 1984 and created designs for Hacker conferences such as H.O.P.E. (Hackers On Planet Earth) RECON (Reverse Engineering conference) and HackFest. He worked as a web developper for some time, creating charactercreator.org and worked on Ages of Empire IV as an interface designer for a short while before returning to his first love of comics with this short story.

Ratel Ancestors is a glimpse into the history of Ratel, a character that will make his first appearance in his own comic title Ratel #1 this summer.
