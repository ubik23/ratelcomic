Bankster

Code Name: BankMan
Filename: Unknown
Specialty: Extracting wealth
Birthplace: Unknown

BankMan is part of the 1%. His wealth and power attracts the most discerning men and women with ambitions that only he can fulfill.

No one knows for sure the full depth of his wealth and power, but no one doubts it is spectacular. The common people don't even know of his existence as he chooses to keep his name and likeness out of the papers.

BankMan enjoys having brunch on the rooftop of his penthouse before boarding his Agusta helicopter to fly off to the first meeting of the day.
