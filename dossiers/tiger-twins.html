Manhunters

Code Name: Tiger Twins
Filename: Omoto and Otomo Yaoguai (虎妖)
Specialty: Tracking and catching Prey
Birthplace: Foshan, China

Little is known of the brothers, other than the fact that they work in tandem for various high profile clients across the world. Their skills are unmatched, and stories of their exploits are heard only in the form of whispers among powerful men.

Little is known about them are they keep an extremely low profile, leaving not a single piece of evidence that could lead back to them, or worst, their client.

One thing is known about these two brothers, they don't suffer disrecpect or dishoner very well. Be warned.

"If you ever see these guys in person, know that it's already too late for you"
