Landlady

Code Name: Mamie
Filename: Himitsu No Supai
Specialty: Getting paid on time
Birthplace: Himeji, Japan

Mamie is a retired elderly woman who lives a quiet life alone with her two cats. She lost her sight years ago in an accident at work which pushed her into an early retirement with a good pension.

She lives alone in a large house and is looking for a lodger who can help with chores around the house and help her generate extra income by renting out an extra room.

Mamie had a 40 year long carreer in espionage. She is retired with an above average pension. She takes on a lodger who can also help her around the house with the things her blindness prevents her to do. Despite her disability and her age, she is ressourceful and has quite a lot of agency for a little old lady.

Incidentaly, she is one of the few remaining members of the Order of the Lotus Dragon.

"She's a lovely old lady, but don't get on her bad side, because when she bites, she goes for the jugular" - A friendly neighbor
