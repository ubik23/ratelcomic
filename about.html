Ratel is an independant Superhero comic book project. It was created by Frederic Guimont in earlly 2023 and the first issue of the comic series will be coming out in 2024 (The launch date will be announced when the artwork nears the end of production). Stay updated on the development with our up to date progress page. I will be updating the numbers on that page as the work is completed.

The comic will be available for preorder shortly through crowdfunding on FMC as well as on Geyser. The marketing campaign is being worked on at the moment and will be announced once everything is ready to go.
